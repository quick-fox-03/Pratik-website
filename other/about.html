Meet Pratik

Hey friend, welcome to my corner of the internet! Happy to have you here, so let's explore together.

I am Pratik Anand, a fellow programming student, an aspiring web developer, a fan of movies, video game enthusiast and intermittent artist.

My story

It's hard to decide where to begin my story.

I come from the city of Gwalior, state of Madhya Pradesh, India. After graduating high school from Delhi Public School, I went on to pursue a Bachelor of Technology degree in the field of Computer Science and Engineering at VIT University, Vellore.
After some basic programming knowledge, I soon delved into the world of web development, as I wanted to build something that I could interact with, that I could see and say "Yes, I built this stuff". And throughout time, I have learnt that the best way of learning programming is just by building things.

I mean, can anyone truly learn how to build houses out of Lego, if they keep only learning how the bricks are made?
Can they learn how to sketch beautiful arts, when they just keep learning how a pencil can create lines?

I still have a lot to learn, a lot to do, and this story is far from concluded. I will keep this site updated, with whatever interesting stuff that happens.
